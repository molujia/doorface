About VocSeek

About Us

VocSeek AI — Powered by affiliated entities in the United States and China

Company Introduction

VocSeek AI is a technology platform dedicated to helping global e-commerce businesses deliver smarter and more efficient customer support.

Our AI-driven after-sales platform enables merchants to manage customer interactions across multiple channels—such as marketplace messages, email, and phone support—through a single unified system. By combining automation with structured product knowledge and service workflows, VocSeek helps support teams respond faster, reduce operational workload, and maintain consistent service quality.

VocSeek is developed and operated by affiliated entities in the United States and China, bringing together expertise in artificial intelligence, cross-border commerce, and customer-service technology.

Our Mission

"Help global e-commerce sellers deliver higher-quality after-sales service with greater efficiency and lower cost — using intelligent tools to elevate customer experience and brand competitiveness."

Core Capabilities

Multi-Channel After-Sales Support

Unified management of Amazon in-app messages, emails, phone calls, SMS, and other after-sales channels for centralized processing.

AI-Assisted Customer Service

Based on product knowledge, historical after-sales data, and order information, providing intelligent reply suggestions, automated processing, and knowledge assistance.

Order-Linked After-Sales Management

Automatically associates each after-sales message with the corresponding order, making communication more efficient and information more accurate.

Knowledge Base & Data Insights

AI automatically processes reviews, conversation records, and product descriptions to continuously optimize the knowledge base and help sellers improve product and service quality.

Secure & Compliant Data Processing

We strictly comply with Amazon's data usage policies, accessing only the data necessary to deliver functionality, and employ encryption and access control measures to ensure data security. User data is never shared with any third party without authorization.

Compliance Commitment

VocSeek AI was designed from the ground up with privacy, security, and transparency as core principles. We strictly adhere to Amazon's developer data specifications, ensuring that data collection, use, storage, and deletion all meet industry-consistent standards.

Our Vision

"To become the preferred intelligent after-sales solution provider for global sellers — empowering every seller to achieve brand growth and global service capability through more professional and intelligent means."

VocSeek AI — 由天津声索科技有限公司提供技术支持

公司介绍

VocSeek AI 是一个致力于帮助全球电子商务企业提供更智能、更高效客户支持的技术平台。

我们的 AI 驱动售后平台使商家能够通过单一的统一系统管理跨多个渠道的客户互动——例如平台消息、电子邮件和电话支持。通过将自动化与结构化的产品知识和服务工作流程相结合，VocSeek 帮助支持团队更快响应、减少运营工作量并保持一致的服务质量。

VocSeek 由美国和中国的关联实体开发和运营，汇集了人工智能、跨境商务和客户服务技术方面的专业知识。

我们的使命

"帮助全球电商卖家以更高效率、更低成本提供更高质量的售后服务——用智能工具提升客户体验与品牌竞争力。"

核心能力

多渠道售后支持

统一管理亚马逊站内信、邮件、电话、短信等售后渠道，集中处理所有客户互动。

AI 辅助客服

基于产品知识、历史售后数据和订单信息，提供智能回复建议、自动化处理和知识辅助。

订单关联售后管理

自动将每条售后消息与对应订单关联，让沟通更高效、信息更准确。

知识库与数据洞察

AI 自动处理评论、对话记录和产品描述，持续优化知识库，帮助卖家提升产品和服务质量。

安全合规的数据处理

严格遵守亚马逊数据使用政策，仅访问实现功能所必需的数据，采用加密和访问控制措施确保数据安全。未经授权，用户数据绝不与任何第三方共享。

合规承诺

VocSeek AI 从设计之初便将隐私、安全和透明度作为核心原则。我们严格遵守亚马逊开发者数据规范，确保数据的收集、使用、存储和删除均符合行业一致标准。

我们的愿景

"成为全球卖家首选的智能售后解决方案提供商——通过更专业、更智能的方式，赋能每一位卖家实现品牌增长与全球化服务能力。"
