FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with VocSeek?
You can start using VocSeek by creating an account. Once registered, you can connect your Amazon store, configure the AI customer service, and begin using the platform. Feel free to contact us if you need any assistance.
Which platforms does VocSeek support?
VocSeek currently focuses on the Amazon platform and supports integration with Amazon Seller Central. Support for additional platforms is on our roadmap.
How is my data kept secure?
VocSeek uses end-to-end encryption and follows the principle of minimal permissions to ensure your data is safe. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and Amazon's data protection policies. For more details, please visit our Data Security page.
How can I get technical support?
You can reach our technical team by email at support@vocseek.ai. We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent issues, we prioritize faster resolution.

常见问题解答

快速了解常见问题的答案

如何开始使用 VocSeek？
您可以通过注册账号开始使用 VocSeek。注册后，您可以关联您的亚马逊店铺，设置 AI 客服，然后开始使用。如需帮助，请随时联系我们。
VocSeek 支持哪些平台？
目前 VocSeek 主要支持亚马逊平台，可以接入亚马逊 Seller Central。更多平台支持正在规划中。
数据安全如何保障？
VocSeek 采用端到端加密技术，遵循最小权限原则，确保您的数据安全。我们符合 GDPR、CCPA 等数据保护法规。更多详情请查看我们的数据安全页面。
